## Overview

BeatPass handles two types of financial transactions: payments from buyers and payouts to producers. All transactions are processed securely through Stripe.

<Info>
  **Audience:** Artists (buyers) and Producers (sellers)
</Info>

---

## For Buyers: Making Payments

### Payment Methods

BeatPass accepts:

- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Digital wallets where available

### Checkout Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add to Cart">
    Select the license tier you want to purchase.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Order">
    Confirm track details, license type, and pricing.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Payment">
    Provide payment details via secure Stripe checkout.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Purchase">
    Complete the transaction and receive confirmation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Downloads">
    Immediately download files and license certificate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="PCI Compliance">
    All payment processing is PCI DSS compliant through Stripe.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="No Stored Card Data">
    BeatPass never stores your full card details. Stripe handles all sensitive data.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Encryption">
    All transactions use TLS encryption.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Receipts & Records

After each purchase:

- Email receipt sent automatically
- Transaction appears in your account history
- License certificate available for download
- Records accessible for tax purposes

## For Producers: Receiving Payouts

### Earning Revenue

Producers earn through:

| Source | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Contribution Pool | Share of subscription revenue based on engagement |
| License Sales | Direct payment from beat purchases |

### Payout Requirements

Before receiving payouts:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set Up Stripe Connect">
    Link or create a Stripe account for receiving funds.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Verification">
    Provide identity verification as required by Stripe.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Payout Method">
    Connect a bank account or debit card.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Meet Minimum Threshold">
    Accumulate at least the minimum payout amount.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Payout Schedule

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Calculation Period | Monthly (1st to end of month) |
| Processing | First week of following month |
| Arrival | 2-5 business days after processing |
| Minimum | Varies by region |

<Note>
  Contribution earnings are calculated after the month ends and processed in the following payout cycle.
</Note>

### Payout Dashboard

Track your earnings in **Backstage > Finances**:

- Current balance
- Pending payouts
- Payout history
- Earnings breakdown by source

## Fees & Splits

### Platform Fees

| Transaction Type | Fee Structure |
|------------------|---------------|
| License Sales | Platform takes percentage of sale |
| Contribution Payouts | Paid from contribution pool |

<Tip>
  Exact fee percentages are detailed in your Producer Agreement.
</Tip>

### Stripe Fees

Standard Stripe processing fees apply to payouts. These are deducted automatically.

## Tax Information

### For Producers

- You are responsible for reporting income
- BeatPass may issue tax forms based on earnings
- Keep records of all payouts received
- Consult a tax professional for your jurisdiction

### For Buyers

- Purchases may include applicable taxes
- Receipts provided for business expense tracking
- Digital goods tax rules vary by location

<Warning>
  BeatPass does not provide tax advice. Consult with a qualified tax professional.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payment declined">
    Check card details, available balance, and try again. Contact your bank if issues persist.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Payout not received">
    Verify your Stripe Connect setup. Check payout history for status. Allow 2-5 business days after processing.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Missing earnings">
    Contribution calculations complete after the month ends. License sales process within 24-48 hours.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Wrong amount">
    Review the earnings breakdown in your dashboard. Contact support if discrepancies remain.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Refunds & Disputes

### Buyer Refunds

Digital licenses are generally non-refundable. Exceptions may apply for:

- Technical issues preventing download
- Duplicate purchases
- Unauthorized transactions

### Producer Disputes

If you believe earnings are incorrect:

1. Review your analytics dashboard
2. Check contribution calculation dates
3. Contact support with specific details
