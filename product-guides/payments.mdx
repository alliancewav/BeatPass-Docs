## Overview

BeatPass handles two types of financial transactions: payments from buyers and payouts to producers. All transactions are processed securely through **Stripe** and **Stripe Connect**.

<Info>
  **Audience:** Artists (buyers) and Producers (sellers)
</Info>

---

## For Buyers: Making Payments

### Payment Methods

BeatPass accepts:

- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- Apple Pay and Google Pay (where supported by browser)

### Per-Track Purchase Flow

BeatPass uses a per-track purchase modelâ€”no cart or checkout page. Each track is purchased individually.

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Track">
    Navigate to the track page you want to license.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select License">
    Choose your license tier (Non-Exclusive or Exclusive).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Initiate Purchase">
    Click **Buy License**. The system calculates the final price including any platform fees.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Payment">
    Complete payment via Stripe's secure checkout (card or digital wallet).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Payment is processed. License is generated automatically.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Files">
    Download track files and your license certificate immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  There is no shopping cart. Each license purchase is a separate transaction.
</Note>

### Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="PCI Compliance">
    All payment processing is PCI DSS compliant through Stripe.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="No Stored Card Data">
    BeatPass never stores your full card details. Stripe handles all sensitive data.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Encryption">
    All transactions use TLS encryption.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Receipts & Records

After each purchase:

- Email receipt sent automatically
- Transaction appears in your account history
- License certificate available for download
- Records accessible for tax purposes

## For Producers: Receiving Payouts

### Earning Revenue

Producers earn through:

| Source | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Contribution Pool** | Share of subscription revenue based on engagement |
| **License Sales** | Direct payment from track purchases via Stripe Connect |

### Stripe Connect Onboarding

<Warning>
  You must complete Stripe Connect onboarding before you can receive payouts from license sales.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Finances">
    Go to **Backstage > Finances** or **Settings > Payments**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Onboarding">
    Click **Connect with Stripe** to begin the Stripe Connect setup.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Verification">
    Stripe requires:
    - Legal name and date of birth
    - Address verification
    - Tax identification (SSN in US, SIN in Canada, etc.)
    - Bank account for payouts
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Return to BeatPass">
    After completing Stripe's flow, you're redirected back. Your account status updates automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Onboarding Status

Check your onboarding status via:

```http
GET /api/v1/artist/stripe-connect/status
```

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `not_started` | Haven't begun onboarding |
| `pending` | Onboarding in progress |
| `complete` | Ready to receive payouts |
| `restricted` | Account needs attention |

### Payout Flow for License Sales

When a buyer purchases your track:

1. **Payment collected** - Stripe processes the buyer's payment
2. **Fees deducted** - Platform fee and Stripe processing fees removed
3. **Transfer initiated** - Remaining amount transferred to your Stripe Connect account
4. **Payout to bank** - Stripe pays out to your bank per your schedule

### Payout Schedule

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **License Sales** | Transferred immediately after purchase (minus fees) |
| **Contribution Pool** | Monthly calculation, processed first week of following month |
| **Bank Payout** | Per your Stripe settings (typically 2-5 business days) |

### Payout Dashboard

Track your earnings in **Backstage > Finances**:

- Stripe Connect status
- License sale transactions
- Subscription contribution earnings
- Payout history
- Unified transaction view

## Fees & Splits

### Platform Fees

| Transaction Type | Fee Structure |
|------------------|---------------|
| License Sales | Platform takes percentage of sale |
| Contribution Payouts | Paid from contribution pool |

<Tip>
  Exact fee percentages are detailed in your Producer Agreement.
</Tip>

### Stripe Fees

Standard Stripe processing fees apply to payouts. These are deducted automatically.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payment declined">
    Check card details, available balance, and try again. Contact your bank if issues persist.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Payout not received">
    Verify your Stripe Connect setup. Check payout history for status. Allow 2-5 business days after processing.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Missing earnings">
    Contribution calculations complete after the month ends. License sales process within 24-48 hours.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Wrong amount">
    Review the earnings breakdown in your dashboard. Contact support if discrepancies remain.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Refunds & Disputes

### Buyer Refunds

Digital licenses are generally non-refundable. Exceptions may apply for:

- Technical issues preventing download
- Duplicate purchases
- Unauthorized transactions

### Producer Disputes

If you believe earnings are incorrect:

1. Review your analytics dashboard
2. Check contribution calculation dates
3. Contact support with specific details
