## Overview

BeatPass includes a direct messaging system for communication between users. Connect with producers, artists, and collaborators without leaving the platform.

<Info>
  **Audience:** All users
</Info>

---

## Accessing Messages

Find your messages:

- Click the message icon in the navigation
- Access from your profile menu
- Reply to notifications

---

## Starting a Conversation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the User">
    Navigate to their profile or track page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Message">
    Look for the message button on their profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Your Message">
    Compose and send your message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Response">
    Notifications alert you when they reply.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Messaging Best Practices

### Professional Communication

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Do">
    - Be clear and specific
    - Introduce yourself briefly
    - State your purpose upfront
    - Respect response times
    - Keep messages concise
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Don't">
    - Send spam or bulk messages
    - Share personal contact info immediately
    - Be pushy or aggressive
    - Send inappropriate content
    - Harass users who don't respond
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Initial Outreach Templates

**For Artists Contacting Producers:**

> Hi [Name], I came across your beat "[Track Name]" and love the vibe. I'm working on a project that could be a great fit. Would you be open to discussing licensing options?

**For Producers Responding to Requests:**

> Hi [Name], I saw your beat request for [description]. I have some tracks that might work for you. Check out [link] and let me know if any of these fit what you're looking for.

**For Collaboration Inquiries:**

> Hey [Name], I really dig your production style. I think our sounds could complement each other well. Would you be interested in collaborating on a track?

## Message Notifications

### Notification Settings

Control how you're notified:

| Channel | Options                       |
| ------- | ----------------------------- |
| In-App  | Badge on message icon         |
| Email   | Instant, daily digest, or off |
| Push    | If mobile enabled             |

### Managing Notifications

1. Go to **Settings \> Notifications**
2. Find Message preferences
3. Toggle channels on/off
4. Set digest frequency

---

## Safety & Guidelines

### Community Standards

All messages must follow platform guidelines:

- No harassment or threats
- No spam or promotional abuse
- No sharing illegal content
- Respect privacy and boundaries

### Reporting Issues

If you receive inappropriate messages:

1. Do not respond
2. Use the report function
3. Block the user if needed
4. Contact support for serious issues

<Warning>
  Violations of messaging guidelines may result in account restrictions or termination.
</Warning>

---

## Limitations

Current messaging limitations:

- Text-based messages only (no file attachments)
- No group messaging
- No read receipts
- Messages with blocked users are hidden
