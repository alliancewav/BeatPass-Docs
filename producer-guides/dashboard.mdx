## Overview

The **Producer Dashboard** is your command center for managing and growing your music business on BeatPass. Access comprehensive analytics, track your progress, manage finances, and compete with other producers.

<Info>
  **Location:** `/producer-dashboard`

  **Requirement:** Approved Producer account with at least one artist profile
</Info>

---

## Accessing Your Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    Click **Producer Dashboard** in the main navigation, or visit `/producer-dashboard` directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Your Overview">
    The home page shows key metrics, recent activity, and quick actions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Sections">
    Use the sidebar to navigate between Progress, Activity, Finances, Leaderboards, Tracks, and Albums.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Finances

Manage your earnings and Stripe Connect integration.

**Location:** `/producer-dashboard/finances`

### Stripe Connect Setup

To receive earnings, you must connect a Stripe account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Finances">
    Go to **Producer Dashboard \> Finances**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Stripe">
    Click **Connect Stripe Account** to begin onboarding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Verification">
    Follow Stripe's onboarding process:

    - Verify identity
    - Add bank account
    - Confirm business information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ready to Earn">
    Once verified, you'll see your dashboard with balance and transactions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  **Required for Earnings** â€” You cannot receive payouts until Stripe Connect is fully set up and verified. Start this process early to avoid delays.
</Warning>

### Finance Dashboard

Once connected, your finance page displays:

| Section                 | Information                 |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------- |
| **Available Balance**   | Funds ready for payout      |
| **Pending Balance**     | Funds in processing         |
| **Recent Transactions** | Individual earnings events  |
| **Payout History**      | Past transfers to your bank |

### Revenue Sources

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Contribution Pool" icon="chart-pie">
    **Subscription Revenue Share**

    Earn from the monthly subscription pool based on your tracks' performance. Your share is calculated using the [Contribution Formula](/contribution/formula).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Direct Sales" icon="cart-shopping">
    **License Purchases**

    Receive direct payments when artists purchase exclusive licenses for your tracks (minus platform fee).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Transaction Types

| Type                    | Description                            |
| ----------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| **contribution_payout** | Monthly subscription pool distribution |
| **exclusive_sale**      | One-time exclusive license purchase    |
| **platform_fee**        | Platform commission deduction          |
| **transfer**            | Payout to your bank account            |

## Leaderboards

Compete with other producers across multiple categories.

**Location:** `/producer-dashboard/leaderboards`

### Leaderboard Categories

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Overall">
    **Composite Ranking**

    Combined score based on:

    - XP Points (40%)
    - Total Plays (30%)
    - Upload Count (20%)
    - Achievements (10%)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Quality">
    **Metadata Completion**

    Ranked by metadata completeness across your catalog:

    - BPM accuracy
    - Key/Scale information
    - Tags and descriptions
    - Cover art

    <Tip>
      Complete all metadata fields to improve your Quality ranking. See the [Metadata Guide](/producer-guides/metadata) for best practices.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Uploads">
    **Track Count**

    Ranked by:

    - Total tracks published
    - Recent uploads (last 30 days)
    - Consistent upload schedule
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Plays">
    **Engagement**

    Ranked by:

    - Total lifetime plays
    - Average plays per track
    - Recent play momentum
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Earnings">
    **Platform Share**

    Ranked by contribution to the platform:

    - Contribution points earned
    - Track performance in pool
    - Consistent earning
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Collaboration">
    **Teamwork**

    Ranked by collaborative activity:

    - Joint tracks released
    - Unique collaborators
    - Collaborative achievements
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Leaderboard Benefits

## Tracks

Manage all your uploaded tracks from one place.

**Location:** `/producer-dashboard/tracks`

### Track Management Features

| Action        | Description                                       |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| **View All**  | See your complete catalog with filters and search |
| **Edit**      | Update track metadata, pricing, licensing options |
| **Analytics** | View per-track performance metrics                |
| **Archive**   | Hide tracks from public without deleting          |
| **Delete**    | Permanently remove tracks                         |

### Track List Information

Each track displays:

- **Cover Art** thumbnail
- **Title** and artist name
- **Play Count** and save count
- **Upload Date**
- **License Status** (available, sold, etc.)
- **Quick Actions** (edit, view, analytics)

### Filtering and Sorting

Filter your tracks by:

- **Status**: Published, Draft, Archived
- **License Type**: Non-exclusive, Exclusive, Both
- **Date Range**: Upload date
- **Performance**: Sort by plays, saves, or recency

## Albums

Organize your tracks into cohesive collections.

**Location:** `/producer-dashboard/albums`

### Album Management

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Album">
    Click **Create Album** to start a new collection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Details">
    Provide album title, description, cover art, and release date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Tracks">
    Select tracks from your catalog to include in the album.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arrange Order">
    Drag and drop to set the track order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Release your album for listeners to discover.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Album Benefits

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Organization" icon="folder">
    Group related beats into themed collections (e.g., "Summer Vibes Pack", "Dark Trap Vol. 1").
  </Card>
  <Card title="Discovery" icon="compass">
    Albums appear in search and recommendations, increasing visibility for multiple tracks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Branding" icon="palette">
    Create a cohesive brand with matching cover art and themed releases.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cross-Selling" icon="arrows-rotate">
    Listeners who find one track may explore the full album.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Dashboard Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check analytics regularly">
    Visit your Activity page weekly to understand what's working. Look for patterns in your top-performing tracks.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Complete your Stripe setup early">
    Don't wait until you have earnings to set up Stripe Connect. Complete verification now to avoid payout delays.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Focus on quality metadata">
    The Quality leaderboard rewards complete metadata. Well-tagged tracks also perform better in search.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Engage with beat requests">
    Responding to beat requests can generate direct sales and new followers. Check the requests page regularly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Create albums strategically">
    Group tracks by theme, mood, or style. Release albums consistently to build anticipation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
