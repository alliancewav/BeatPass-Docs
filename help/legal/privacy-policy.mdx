## Who We Are

BeatPass is operated by **Alliance Productions Records Inc.** This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the BeatPass platform.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you create an account or use BeatPass, you give us:

- **Account details** — Name, email address, username, password
- **Transaction information** — Details when you subscribe or purchase licenses
- **Communications** — Messages you send to other users or to support
- **Beat request details** — Specifications for custom beat requests

### Information Collected Automatically

When you use BeatPass, we automatically collect:

- **Device information** — IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Usage data** — Pages viewed, beats played, downloads, time spent
- **Cookies** — Small files that help us remember your preferences

### Payment Information

When you make a purchase, our payment processor (Stripe) handles your payment details. **We do not store credit card numbers on our servers.**

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

| Purpose | Examples |
|---------|----------|
| **Run the service** | Manage accounts, process transactions, track licensing |
| **Personalize your experience** | Recommend beats, remember preferences |
| **Improve BeatPass** | Analyze usage patterns, develop new features |
| **Process payments** | Handle subscriptions and purchases |
| **Communicate with you** | Send service updates, respond to support requests |
| **Prevent fraud** | Detect unauthorized access, protect accounts |

## How We Share Your Information

**We do not sell or rent your personal information.**

We may share information with:

- **Service providers** — Companies that help us run BeatPass (hosting, payments, analytics) under confidentiality agreements
- **Producers and consumers** — Transaction data shared as needed for licensing (e.g., producers see aggregate download data for royalty calculations)
- **Legal requirements** — When required by law or to respond to legal processes
- **Business transfers** — If BeatPass is acquired, your information may transfer to the new owner

## Your Messages and Requests

When you send messages through BeatPass or submit beat requests:

- Message content is shared with the recipient(s)
- Beat request details are shared with producers who may fulfill them
- We may review messages to enforce our terms and prevent abuse

## Data Protection

We use industry-standard security measures:

- Encryption of sensitive data
- Secure server environments
- Access controls limiting who can view your information

<Note>
  While we work hard to protect your data, no online platform can guarantee 100% security.
</Note>

## Age Requirements

BeatPass is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information
- Object to certain uses of your information

To exercise these rights, contact us at **contact@beatpass.ca**.

## Cookies

We use cookies to:

- Keep you logged in
- Remember your preferences (like dark mode)
- Understand how you use BeatPass
- Improve our service

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work properly without them.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes through the platform or by email.

If you have questions about our privacy practices:
