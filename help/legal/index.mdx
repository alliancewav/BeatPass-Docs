## Key Legal Points

### For Everyone

- BeatPass is operated by **Alliance Productions Records Inc.**, doing business as BeatPass
- By using BeatPass, you agree to our Terms of Service
- Your privacy matters — we don't sell your personal information
- You must be at least 18 years old to use BeatPass

### For Subscribers

- When you download a beat, you receive a **non-exclusive license** to use it
- Your license is **perpetual** — it doesn't expire, even if you cancel your subscription
- You must **credit the producer** in your releases
- You can use beats in one project per download

### For Producers

- You **keep ownership** of all beats you upload
- BeatPass only receives a license to operate the platform and distribute your beats
- Your beats remain yours even after consumers download them
