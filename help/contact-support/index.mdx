## How to Contact Us

### Contact Page

The easiest way to reach us is through our Contact page:

1. Navigate to the **Contact** page (available in the footer or menu)
2. Fill in your **name** and **email address**
3. Write your **message** describing your question or issue
4. Click **Send Message**

You'll receive a confirmation once your message is submitted, and our team will respond to the email address you provided.

## Response Times

We strive to respond to all inquiries as quickly as possible:

| Priority | Expected Response |
|----------|-------------------|
| **Account access issues** | Within 24 hours |
| **Payment/billing issues** | Within 24 hours |
| **General questions** | 24-48 hours |
| **Feature requests** | 48-72 hours |

<Note>
  Response times are for business days. Messages received on weekends or holidays may take longer to receive a response.
</Note>

## Before Contacting Support

To help us resolve your issue faster, please:

1. **Check this Help Center** — Your question may already be answered
2. **Include relevant details** — Account email, screenshots, error messages
3. **Be specific** — Describe what you were trying to do and what happened
4. **One issue per message** — This helps us track and resolve problems efficiently
