## Platform Roles

BeatPass supports four primary user roles. Each role has specific capabilities and access levels.

---

## Producer

<Card title="Producers" icon="sliders">
  Creators who upload original beats and instrumentals to the platform.
</Card>

### Capabilities

- Upload unlimited beats with metadata (BPM, key, genre, mood)
- Set licensing terms and pricing
- Track plays, saves, completions, and earnings
- Respond to beat requests from artists
- Access Producer Intelligence dashboard
- Earn contribution-based revenue
- Message and collaborate with other users

### Requirements

- Verified email address
- Completed producer profile with at least one track
- Acceptance of Producer Agreement

---

## Artist

<Card title="Artists" icon="microphone">
  Musicians who discover, license, and use beats for their projects.
</Card>

### Capabilities

- Browse and preview the full beat catalog
- Purchase or stream-license beats
- Download licensed tracks with documentation
- Save beats to personal library
- Create and manage playlists
- Message producers directly
- Post beat requests

### Requirements

- Verified email address
- Completed artist profile

---

## Curator

<Card title="Curators" icon="list-music">
  Tastemakers who create playlists and showcase emerging talent.
</Card>

### Capabilities

- Create public and private playlists
- Feature beats and producers
- Build a follower base
- Access curation analytics
- Participate in curator programs (when available)

### Requirements

- Verified email address
- Minimum activity threshold for curator badge

---

## Listener

<Card title="Listeners" icon="headphones">
  Users who explore and enjoy beats without commercial licensing needs.
</Card>

### Capabilities

- Browse and stream the full catalog
- Save favorites to library
- Follow producers and curators
- Create personal playlists
- Contribute to play metrics (supporting producers)

### Requirements

- Free account registration

---

## Role Combinations

Users can hold multiple roles simultaneously:

| Combination | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| Producer + Artist | Create beats and also use others' beats |
| Artist + Curator | License beats and curate playlists |
| Producer + Curator | Upload beats and create showcase playlists |

<Note>
  Upgrading your role is free. Visit your profile settings to enable additional capabilities.
</Note>
