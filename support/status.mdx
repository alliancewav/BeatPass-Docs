## Incident Communication

### How We Communicate

During incidents, we communicate through:

1. **Status Page** - Real-time updates
2. **In-App Banners** - For widespread issues
3. **Social Media** - Major announcements
4. **Email** - For affected users if needed

### Incident Levels

| Level | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Operational | All systems normal | - |
| Degraded | Reduced performance | Slow loading times |
| Partial Outage | Some features unavailable | Uploads disabled |
| Major Outage | Core functionality down | Site unreachable |

## Scheduled Maintenance

### When We Schedule Maintenance

- Non-urgent: Announced 48+ hours in advance
- Urgent: Announced as soon as possible
- Emergency: May occur without advance notice

### Maintenance Windows

Preferred maintenance times:
- Tuesday-Thursday
- 2:00 AM - 6:00 AM EST (off-peak hours)

---

## What to Do During Issues

### During Degraded Performance

- Wait and retry after a few minutes
- Check status page for updates
- Avoid repeated actions (can worsen issues)

### During Outages

- Check status page for estimated resolution
- Do not attempt to re-upload or re-process
- Wait for confirmation before resuming

<Warning>
  Avoid repeated upload or payment attempts during outages to prevent duplicates.
</Warning>
