## Overview

BeatPass tracks various metrics to help producers understand their performance and earnings. This guide defines each metric and explains how it's calculated.

<Info>
  **Audience:** Producers
</Info>

---

## Engagement Metrics

### Plays

**Definition:** The total number of times your tracks have been streamed.

| Type | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Total Plays | All streaming events |
| Unique Plays | Distinct users who played |
| Repeat Plays | Same user playing again |

### Completions

**Definition:** Plays that reach at least 80% of the track duration.

- Completions indicate genuine interest
- Weighted higher in contribution calculations
- High completion rate signals quality

### Completion Rate

**Definition:** Percentage of plays that become completions.

```
Completion Rate = (Completions / Total Plays) × 100
```

| Rating | Range |
|--------|-------|
| Excellent | 70%+ |
| Good | 50-70% |
| Average | 30-50% |
| Low | Below 30% |

### Saves

**Definition:** Number of users who added your track to their library.

- Indicates intent to return
- Stronger engagement signal than plays
- Contributes to discovery algorithms

### Save Rate

**Definition:** Percentage of listeners who saved the track.

```
Save Rate = (Saves / Unique Plays) × 100
```

## Performance Metrics

### Contribution Value

**Definition:** Your calculated share of the platform revenue pool.

- Based on engagement metrics
- Relative to total platform engagement
- Updates hourly

<Card title="Contribution Formula" icon="calculator" href="/contribution/formula">
  Learn how contribution value is calculated.
</Card>

### Estimated Earnings

**Definition:** Projected payout based on current contribution value.

- Estimate only, not guaranteed
- Final amount determined at month end
- Depends on total pool and platform engagement

### Quality Score

**Definition:** Composite score based on metadata completeness and audio quality.

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Complete metadata | Positive |
| High audio bitrate | Positive |
| Cover art present | Positive |
| Missing BPM/Key | Negative |

## Audience Metrics

### Listener Demographics

Where available, see breakdowns of your audience:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Geographic | Top listening regions |
| Platform | Web vs mobile |
| Source | How listeners found your tracks |

### Follower Count

**Definition:** Users who follow your producer profile.

- Followers see your new uploads in their feed
- Indicates audience building progress
- Quality of followers matters more than quantity

### Follower Growth

**Definition:** Net change in followers over time.

```
Growth Rate = (New Followers - Lost Followers) / Previous Total × 100
```

## Track-Level Metrics

### Per-Track Analytics

Each track shows:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Total Plays | Lifetime stream count |
| Completions | Full listens |
| Saves | Library additions |
| Peak Day | Highest single-day plays |
| Trend | Rising, stable, or declining |

### Track Performance Score

**Definition:** Calculated score combining multiple signals.

```
Performance = (Plays × Weight) + (Completions × Weight) + (Saves × Weight)
```

Exact weights are not disclosed to prevent gaming.

## Time-Based Metrics

### Time Periods

Analytics can be viewed across:

| Period | Use Case |
|--------|----------|
| 24 Hours | Immediate performance |
| 7 Days | Weekly trends |
| 30 Days | Monthly performance |
| 90 Days | Quarterly analysis |
| Lifetime | Overall performance |

### Trend Indicators

| Indicator | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| Rising | Metric increasing vs previous period |
| Stable | Metric relatively unchanged |
| Declining | Metric decreasing vs previous period |
